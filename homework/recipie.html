cookies

follow the steps below to bake the perfect cookies.


ingredients:

  • 125g unsalted butter
  • 100g light brown sugar
  • 75g white granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla (optional)
  • 300g plain flour
  • 1.5 tsp baking powder
  • 0.5 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 0.5 tsp sea salt
  • 300g chocolate chips

Instructions:

Remember to preheat the oven to 180C

  1. step 1:
    • Add your butter and sugars to a bowl and beat till creamy
  2. step 2:
    • Add in your egg and beat again
    • if using vanilla, add it in now!
  3. Step 3:
    • Add in the:
      1. plain flour
      2. Baking powder
      3. Bicarbonate of soda
      4. Salt
      and beat until the dough is formed.
  4. Step 4:
    • Add in your chocolate chips and beat till distributed well and roll into cookie dough balls.
  5. Step 5:
    • Once rolled into balls put your cookie dough in the freezer for 30 minutes
  6. Step 6:
    • Take your cookies out the freezer and put them onto a lined baking tray
  7. Step 7:
    • Bake in the oven for 12-14 minutes
  8. Step 8:
    • Once baked leave them to cool on the tray for 30 mins as they will continue to bake whilst cooling

  9. ENJOY YOUR FRESHLY BAKED COOKIES!!
